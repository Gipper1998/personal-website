About_Me

Hello, my name is Trent Halama and I am currently in my senior year at Florida Polytechnic University seeking a Bachelors Degree in Computer Engineering. Originally grew up in the Florida Keys and previously attended Florida Institute of Technology and College of the Florida Keys.

Throughout my journey outside academics, I have been on lots of adventures outside academics like being a Resident Assistant, and currently an Orientation Leader and serving on the Florida Polycon board as Vice-President of Vendors.

In my studies I have done projects ranging with communicating with Arduino using registers, autonomous robotic vechicles, and a wide range of coding projects. I am continuing to learn every day and I continue to make progress on achieving all my goals and making new ones every single day.

Projects

There have been a lot of projects I've been apart of for academics as well as personal use to help on future projects.   • 1 x Arduino Uno Rev 3

Experience

Florida Polycon

Florida Polycon is a student-run organization under the guidance of Florida Polytechnic University's Student Government Association that runs an annual convention-style event that brings in over 2000 visitors to the University. I started in 2022 as the Vendor-Events Coordinator, communicating with all vendors such as Fireworks, Food Trucks, and so much more. Now I serve as the Vice President of Vendors for 2023, which directs all vendors in the exhibitor space to conclude my senior year at the University.

Intern at Nettally

To gain real-world knowledge and experience, I interned for Nettally (Network Tallahassee), a small company located in Tallahassee that helps provide internet, email, and phone service to their community in 2023. While learning some network terminology, I helped develop a website for the company (www.nettally.com) and helped with customer relations.

Orientation Leader

In the Fall of 2023, I became an Orientation Leader for the University under the New Student and Family Program at Florida Polytechnic University. During this process, I helped the newly incoming class of 2027 transition from the High School to the University lifestyle by letting them know the resources available if they need any assistance. In addition, answering any questions they have as well as touch base with them throughout their first semester at the University.

Resident Assistant

At the Florida Insitute of Technology for the Fall of 2021 and Spring of 2022, I became a Resident Assistant (RA) of Roberts Hall, a dorm that only allows first-year students. I served as a role model throughout the dorms answering any questions, maintaining policies, and running small events to bring awareness of various topics such as diversity and campus resources. I also was a part of helping transition the first years from High School to the University lifestyle.

Games

Snake

Navigate around a small box and collect all the red apple (or squares) around the board until you die. Remember, don't hit the wall, or yourself for that matter.

Connect 4

A classic, grap your circles and get 4 in a row in any direction. Blocking is the key, so keep a good eye on the board before your opponent wins (must have 2 players to play).

Tic Tac Toe

Get 3 in a row in any direction, first one gets it wins (must have 2 players to play).

Memory

Cards will be displayed and you must find a match, if you find them all, you win the game!
